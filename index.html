How it works.
▶
Hit Play to start your track. Waveform listens to your audio in real time and generates visuals as it plays. Recording starts automatically — just let it run.
◈
Switch between Basic, Animals, Flowers, Galactic or Random using the style pills at the bottom. Change anytime — each style reacts differently to your music.
⚡
Color toggles a colour palette on and off. Burst fires a burst of shapes from the center — great for drops. Clear wipes the canvas and starts fresh.
↓
When you're done, stop the track and you'll be prompted to download your video. It's ready to post on TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, or Spotify Canvas.
