# ⚡ Waveform

**Turn your music into visual art. Preview free — download for $2.**

Waveform is an audio visualizer that transforms any music track into stunning geometric animations in real time. Upload a track, watch it come alive, and download your video.

**Live:** [waveform.rocks](https://waveform.rocks)

---

## What It Does

Upload an MP3, WAV, AAC, or M4A file and Waveform instantly generates a synchronized visual experience — geometric shapes, animals, flowers, or cosmic patterns that pulse and react to every beat, drop, and frequency in your track.

Move your cursor through the visuals and watch the shapes scatter and respond. Everything renders locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server.

---

## Visual Styles

- **Basic** — triangles, hexagons, stars, crosses, and line geometry
- **Animals** — line-drawn birds, fish, butterflies, wolves, and whales
- **Flowers** — petal blooms, lotus formations, spirals, and spirograph patterns
- **Galactic** — planets with rings, orbiting moons, comets, galaxies, and starbursts
- **Random** — cycles through all styles dynamically for a surprise every time

---

## How It Works

1. **Enter your details** — name, artist/band, and video title
2. **Upload your track** — MP3, WAV, AAC, or M4A
3. **Preview free** — watch the full visualization, try every style, move your cursor through the shapes
4. **Download for $2** — clean MP4 video, no watermark, no subscription

## Controls

| Control | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Play / Pause** | Start or pause playback |
| **Restart** | Restart from the beginning |
| **Color** | Toggle color mode on/off |
| **Burst** | Explode shapes from cursor position |
| **Basic / Animals / Flowers / Galactic / Random** | Switch visual style |
| **Clear** | Reset and start over |
