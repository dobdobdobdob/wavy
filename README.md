# ⚡ Waveform

**Turn your music into visual art. Preview free — download for $2.**

Waveform is an AI-powered audio visualizer that transforms any music track into stunning geometric animations in real time. Upload a track, watch it come alive, and download your video.

---

## What It Does

Upload any MP3, WAV, or AAC file and Waveform instantly generates a synchronized visual experience — geometric shapes, animals, flowers, or cosmic patterns that pulse and react to every beat, drop, and frequency in your track.

Move your cursor through the visuals and watch the shapes scatter and respond. Everything renders locally in your browser — nothing is uploaded to a server.

---

## Visual Styles

- **Basic** — triangles, hexagons, stars, crosses, and line geometry
- **Animals** — line-drawn birds, fish, butterflies, wolves, and whales
- **Flowers** — petal blooms, lotus formations, spirals, and spirograph patterns
- **Galactic** — planets with rings, orbiting moons, comets, galaxies, and starbursts

---

## How It Works

1. **Enter your details** — name, artist/band, and video title
2. **Upload your track** — any MP3, WAV, or AAC file
3. **Preview free** — watch the full visualization, try every style, move your cursor through the shapes
4. **Download for $2** — clean 1080p MP4, no watermark, no subscription

---

## Built With

- Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks
- Web Audio API for real-time frequency analysis
- Canvas API for geometric rendering
- Claude AI for development
- Cloudflare Pages for hosting
- Stripe for payments (coming soon)

## Why I Built This

YouTubers, musicians, and content creators spend hours searching for visualizers for lyric videos, lo-fi streams, and social content. Most tools are subscription-based, complicated, or produce generic output.

Waveform is the simplest possible version: upload, preview, pay once, download. No account needed. No monthly fee. Your music, your art.
